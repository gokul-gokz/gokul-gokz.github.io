Within manipulation using Variable friction finger gripper

Developed a hybrid method integrating motion planner and online visual servoing to perform within hand manipulation of objects using variable friction finger gripper. Evaluated the performance of the system with inaccuracies in the modelling of the system and uncertainities in the motion of the object. Submitted the work as a research paper in ICRA 2020 which is under review. Currently working on developing a reinforcement learning algorithm to manipulate objects without the need for explicit modelling.
